🏫 School Gap

A 19-point gap exists between private (69%) and public (50%) school AI guidance — the largest disparity we found.

👪 Parent Role

Fewer than half of parents discuss AI risks. Parental guidance is nearly equal across school types.

📊 Correlation

Students who received guidance scored 0.4 points higher on risk awareness — a significant and meaningful difference.

What Should We Do?

Our recommendations for parents, schools, and policymakers.

🎓

Integrate AI Literacy

Schools should embed AI risk education into existing curricula — not as a one-off assembly, but as an ongoing conversation across subjects.

👨‍👩‍👧

Empower Parents

Provide parents with accessible resources and conversation guides so they can discuss AI risks at home, regardless of their own tech literacy.

🏛️

Close the Equity Gap

Public schools need dedicated funding and resources for AI education — the current 19-point gap with private schools is unacceptable.

🔬

Fund More Research

This is only the beginning. Longitudinal studies are needed to track how risk perception evolves as AI becomes more embedded in teens' lives.
